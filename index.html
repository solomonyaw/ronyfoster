Who is Rony Foster?

Foster Kissi Asante, better known or identified by his stage name Rony Foster, is a Ghanaian-Italian rapper and song writer born in Koforidua, Ghana but now based in Brescia, Italy.

He rose to fame in Italy in 2016 when he got nominated for the category ”best music video” with his single, “No Mercy” during a ceremony held by African Royal Awards (ARA). He has also performed on stage in Italy with great Ghanaian artists like Medikal, KiDi, Fameye and Stonebwoy.

Though Rony is yet to release an album (which is a work in progress to be released this year), he has worked on a couple of singles which are available on most digital music platforms such as Apple Music, Soundcloud, Audiomack, YouTube and the likes.

He has recently released a fresh hip-life single titled “warm up” which he has personally confirmed is providing a good feedback from his fans both in Italy and Ghana who felt in love with the tune.
