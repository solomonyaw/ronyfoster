About me

Foster Kissi Asante, better known or identified by his stage name Rony Foster, is a Ghanaian-Italian rapper and song writer born in Koforidua,Ghana but now based in Brescia,Italy. He rose to fame in Italy in 2016 when he got nominated for the category ”best music video” with his single, “No Mercy” during a ceremony held by African Royal Awards (ARA). He has also performed on stage in Italy with great Ghanaian artists like Medikal, KiDi ,Fameye and Stonebwoy. Though Rony is yet to release an album (which is a work in progress to be released this year), he has workedon a couple of singles which are available on most digital music platforms such as Apple Music, Soundcloud ,Audiomack, YouTube and the likes. He has recently released a fresh hip-life single titled “warm up” which he has personally confirmed is providing a good feedback from his fans both in Italy and Ghana who felt in love with the tune.

RFNFT

Technology has been a 7th wonder to music playing the most vital role in its evolution and advancement. It has in fact inspired diverse ways to create, listen and commercialize music and art. The domination of Non-Fungible Tokens better known as NFT’s in the mainstream gifts a bunch of endless options to curator’s trade their art’s digital ownership rights to interested buyers. This commercial window is exciting news to Rony Foster and his music ambitions motivating his move to introducing his own NFT platform dubbed the RFNFT, Rony Foster NFT.Rony hopes to build an NFT platform coarsely for his fans hoping to provide them a chance to own hi digital assets.
